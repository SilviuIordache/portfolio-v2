Need for Speed Heat (stylised as NFS Heat) is a 2019 racing video game developed by Ghost Games and published by Electronic Arts for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. It is the twenty-fourth instalment in the Need for Speed series and commemorates the series' 25th anniversary.

The project was done over a period of 2 year with a team of 200 people from Romania, UK and Sweden: designers, graphical artists, software engineers, QAs and more.

Game Features

  • High-octane racing and story
  • Two main game modes: day sanctione races and illegal night races
  • Single-player and multi-player action
  • Car customization
  • Free-roam open world
  • Challenge AIs or other players to races
  • Cop chase game modes
  • Collectibles challenges
