Assassin's Creed: Pirates is an action adventure game in which you played as a pirate travelling the Carribean seas.

The project was done over a period of 1 year with a team of 30 people from Romania and France: designers, graphical artists, software engineers, QAs and more.

The game was released for iOS, Android, Windows Phone and the browser. It was available for download for 7 years on the mobile marketplaces, until it was eventually taken down. It is still available to download and and install on some websites.

Game Features

  • Beautiful game graphics
  • Sea exploration: sailing, fishing, treasures, rescuing sailors
  • Mini-game 1: First person boat-to-boat combat
  • Mini-game 2: Stealth sailing, attempting to sail a boat undetected
  • Crew assembly and management
  • Ship customization
  • A thrilling story

My work

  • Designed and helped to implement half of the game levels.
  • Implemented game content: Enemy-AI ships, Collectible treasures, air-gusts, etc.
  • Research for real-life locations and landscapes and bringing them in game
  • Created mock-ups for social menu features and in-game-currency shops.
  • Close collaboration with graphical artists, software engineers and QAs to bring fun experiences to live.
