Need for Speed Payback is an open-world racing video game developed by Ghost Games and published by Electronic Arts for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. It is the twenty-third installment in the Need for Speed series.

The project was done over a period of 2 year with a team of 200 people from Romania, UK and Sweden: designers, graphical artists, software engineers, QAs and more.

Game Features

  • High-octane racing and story
  • Single-player and multi-player action
  • Car customization
  • Free-roam open world
  • Challenge AIs or other players to races
  • Cop chase game modes
  • Collectibles challenges

My work

I designed and help implement by collaborating with graphical artists and software engineers some game features and game content:

Roaming Racers
  • This is a free roam game mode where you can find and challenge AI racers to open world races.
  • Researched and came up with the design for the AI cars.
  • Implemented all 16 AI racers in game and fine-tuned the feature to turn it into a fun experience
Multiplayer levels
  • I was responsible for the implementation of all 25+ online game levels
  • Worked closely with QAs and other team members to fine tune this content to provide the best player experience
