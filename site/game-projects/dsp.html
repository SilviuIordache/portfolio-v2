Driver: Speedboat Paradise is a high octane boat racing game where you try to make your way up the world of boat-racing, while collecting and upgrading your vehicles.

The project was done over a period of 1 year with a team of 30 people from Romania and France: designers, graphical artists, software engineers, QAs and more.

The game was released for iOS, Android, Windows Phone and the browser. It was available for download for 5 years on the mobile marketplaces, until it was eventually taken down. It is still available to download and and install on some websites.

Game Features

  • Beautiful game graphics
  • A plethora of race boats to unlock, buy, upgrade, customize and race
  • A thriller story involving the Miami Mafia
  • Multiple game modes: Circuits, Drag Races, Boss Battles

  • Game achievements system
  • Weekly challenges
