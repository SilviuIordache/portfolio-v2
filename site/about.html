About Me

My name is Silviu Iordache, I was born in Craiova, Romania in 1992. I currently work as a full-stack web developer at Caphyon.

My skills include analytic thinking and attention to detail. I enjoy solving algoritmic challenges.

My professional career started in the video game industry, where I worked for almost 6 years as a game designer and content creator for Ubisoft and Electronic Arts , in the Assassin's Creed, Driver and Need for Speed franchises:

  • Need for Speed: Heat (2019 - PC, PS4, XB1)
  • Need for Speed: Payback (2017, PC, PS4, XB1)
  • Driver: Speedboat Paradise (2015, iOS, Android)
  • Assassin's Creed: Pirates (2015, iOS, Android, Web)
